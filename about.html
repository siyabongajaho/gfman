PRODUCT RANGE.

Access to the working area of a mine is often through a number of shaft systems, with working face's some significant distance from the main shaft. Inevitable distance effects the working time at the stope face. The costs of transporting men and materials to and from these working areas will ultimately determine the viability of a mining project.

Solutions to these logistical problems are in the use of :

OUR TEAM OF SKILLED ENGINEERING MANAGEMENT AND HIGHLY QUALIFIED PRODUCTION HUMAN RESOURCES AND PHYSICAL FACILITY.
