Skips & Chutes

  • Dimensions : Width, height and depth vary by model; hopper angle facilitates gravity discharge.
  • Material : Welded steel frame with a yellow powder-coated finish; hopper bottom for dust collection.
  • Airflow : Usually rated in CFM (cubic feet per minute); specific value depends on the model (common ranges 1000-5000 CFM).
  • Inlet/Outlet : Circular flange for duct connection; outlet typically connects to a fan or exhaust.
  • Mantainance : Access door for filter replacement; dust discharge via bottom valve.
